Application

Kota Stone for Industrial Environments

Industrial flooring is subjected to conditions that defeat most materials — heavy machinery loads, chemical spills, temperature extremes, and constant abrasion. Kota Stone, with its exceptional compressive strength (70+ MPa) and extremely low porosity, has been the flooring of choice for Indian industrial facilities for well over a century.

Unlike epoxy coatings that chip and delaminate, or concrete floors that crack and dust, Kota Stone industrial flooring maintains its structural integrity and surface quality indefinitely under continuous industrial use. It resists most industrial chemicals, oils, and solvents, and unlike coated surfaces, a Kota Stone floor never needs stripping and recoating.

Available in 25–30 mm heavy-duty thickness with natural or leather finish for maximum slip resistance, Kota Stone is the only industrial flooring solution that combines practical performance with zero maintenance cost.

Industrial Performance

Why Industry Trusts Kota Stone

Heavy Load Bearing

30 mm Kota Stone flooring withstands forklift traffic, heavy machinery standloads, and stacked pallet storage without cracking or deflection.

Chemical Resistance

Resists dilute acids, oils, greases, and most industrial solvents and chemicals encountered in manufacturing and processing environments.

Maximum Slip Safety

Natural finish provides excellent R12 slip resistance — critical for worker safety compliance in wet or oily industrial environments.

Easy Industrial Cleaning

Can be hosed down, pressure washed, and scrubbed with industrial cleaning agents without damaging the surface — unlike coated floors.

No Replacement Cycle

While epoxy coatings need replacement every 5–7 years, a properly installed Kota Stone floor lasts the entire lifetime of the building.

Cold Storage Compatible

Frost-resistant Kota Stone is used in cold storage facilities and refrigerated warehouses where temperature cycling destroys most floor surfaces.
