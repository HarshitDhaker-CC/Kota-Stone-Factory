Application

Kota Stone Wall Cladding

Kota Stone wall cladding transforms ordinary walls into architectural statements. Cut into 10–12 mm thin slabs, Kota Stone panels can be applied to interior and exterior walls to create dramatic visual effects — from sleek contemporary feature walls to rugged rustic facade treatments.

The natural blue-green and brown tones of Kota Stone bring warmth, texture, and organic beauty to spaces that would otherwise feel cold or clinical. In high-end hospitality, corporate offices, retail environments, and premium residences, Kota Stone cladding creates an instant impression of quality and sophistication.

On building exteriors, Kota Stone cladding provides both aesthetic enhancement and weather protection — the dense limestone acts as an insulating and protective layer, improving the thermal performance and longevity of the underlying wall structure.

Why Kota for Wall Cladding

Key Advantages

Dramatic Visual Impact

Stone cladding instantly elevates any space — creating a visual focal point that commands attention and communicates premium quality.

Lightweight Thin Slabs

At 10–12 mm, the panels are structurally much lighter than full-thickness stone — enabling application to lightweight walls and upper floors with no structural modification.

Natural Insulation

Applied to exterior facades, Kota Stone provides thermal mass that moderates temperature swings, reducing heating and cooling loads in buildings.

Weather & Moisture Resistant

The low porosity of Kota Stone makes it resistant to moisture penetration, staining, and biological growth on external facades.

Multiple Finishes

Choose from polished (high gloss), honed (matte), natural (textured), or leather (brushed) finishes to suit the design character of each project.

Easy Installation

Applied with standard tile adhesive and grout — compatible with conventional tiling techniques and most wall surface types.
