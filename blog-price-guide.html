What Is the Current Price of Kota Stone in 2025?

Kota Stone remains one of India's most cost-effective premium natural flooring materials. In 2025, prices range from ₹18 to ₹120 per sq ft depending on finish type, stone grade, thickness, and whether you're buying raw stone or factory-finished tiles. The wide range exists because "Kota Stone" encompasses everything from raw quarried slabs to mirror-polished premium tiles.

5 Factors That Affect Kota Stone Price

1. Grade of Stone (A, B, C)

Grade A stone is hand-selected for uniform colour, no visible cracks, and consistent thickness. Grade B allows minor natural variation. Grade C includes pieces with some colour variation or slight imperfections, significantly cheaper for non-visible applications like industrial flooring. Always specify the grade when requesting a quote.

2. Colour Variant

Kota Blue (the classic blue-green) is the most widely available and generally priced at the base rate above. Kota Brown is slightly rarer and commands a 10–15% premium. Special cuts, matched veining patterns, or exotic colour selections can push prices 20–30% higher.

3. Transport Distance from Kota

Ex-quarry prices are FOB Ramganj Mandi. Transport costs vary significantly by destination — typically ₹3–8 per sq ft for nearby Rajasthan states, ₹8–18 per sq ft for distant states like Karnataka or West Bengal. Ordering in bulk (10,000+ sq ft) reduces per-unit freight substantially.

4. Custom Sizes and Edge Profiles

Standard 600×600 mm tiles come at base price. Custom dimensions, specific edge profiles (bevelled, bullnose, pencil edge), or non-rectangular cuts for staircases and curved features incur cutting charges of ₹5–20 per linear metre of cut edge.

5. Volume Ordered

Kota Stone pricing is highly volume-sensitive. Orders above 500 sq ft typically qualify for 5–10% discount. Above 5,000 sq ft, discounts of 15–25% are common. Projects above 25,000 sq ft can negotiate directly with the quarry for the best possible rates.

Total Installed Cost — Quick Estimate

For a typical 1,000 sq ft residential living room with honed Kota Blue (20 mm), delivered to Delhi, and installed:

  • Stone material (honed, Grade A, 1,000 sq ft): ₹35,000 – ₹45,000
  • Transport (Kota to Delhi, ~620 km): ₹7,000 – ₹12,000
  • Installation labour: ₹28,000 – ₹40,000
  • Grout, adhesive, sundries: ₹3,000 – ₹5,000
  • Total: ₹73,000 – ₹1,02,000 (~₹73 – ₹102 per sq ft all-in)

Compare this to polished marble (₹150–250/sq ft installed) or vitrified tiles (₹90–160/sq ft installed) — Kota Stone offers superior durability at significantly lower total cost.
