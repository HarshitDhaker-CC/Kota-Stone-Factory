The #1 Choice for Indian Parking Lots

Parking areas face some of the most demanding conditions of any flooring use case — heavy axle loads, oil and fuel spills, constant abrasion, and monsoon water exposure. Kota Stone meets all these challenges without compromise, which is why it remains the dominant parking tile material across commercial, industrial, and residential projects in India.

Unlike concrete pavers that crack under load or ceramic tiles that become dangerously slippery when wet, Kota Stone parking tiles offer natural anti-skid grip even in oily or wet conditions, can handle vehicles weighing tonnes, and require virtually no maintenance over a 25+ year lifespan.

Direct mine sourcing from Ramganj Mandi allows us to supply any volume at consistent quality — from a 200 sq ft home driveway to a 50,000 sq ft commercial parking complex.

Parking Performance

Why Kota Stone for Parking

Six key properties that make Kota Stone the undisputed best parking tile material in India.

Heavy Load Capacity

With compressive strength ≥ 70 MPa and 25–30 mm thickness, Kota Stone supports cars, SUVs, trucks, and even forklift traffic without cracking or deforming.

Natural Anti-Skid Grip

Natural and flamed finishes provide excellent friction — no anti-slip coatings needed. The surface remains safe even when wet, oily, or muddy from monsoon conditions.

Oil & Chemical Resistant

Kota Stone's dense, low-absorption structure resists oil, grease, diesel, brake fluid, and common parking lot chemicals without staining or deteriorating.

Rain & Weather Proof

Less than 0.5% water absorption means water cannot penetrate and cause frost damage or surface spalling — ideal for open-air parking exposed to monsoon rain.

Zero Maintenance

Unlike interlocking pavers that shift and settle, or concrete that cracks, properly laid Kota Stone parking tiles require only occasional water washing for decades of use.

Best Value Per Year

With a 25+ year life expectancy and near-zero maintenance costs, the total cost of ownership of Kota Stone parking tiles is dramatically lower than any alternative.
