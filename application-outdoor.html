Application

Kota Stone for Outdoor Pathways

Outdoor pathways face one of the toughest demands in construction — they must withstand constant weathering, UV exposure, monsoon rains, vehicle loads, and heavy foot traffic while remaining safe, functional, and beautiful year after year. Kota Stone meets all of these demands with ease.

The natural, slightly rough surface provides exceptional anti-slip grip in all weather conditions — eliminating the slip hazard that polished or glazed surfaces create when wet. This makes Kota Stone the standard recommendation for garden pathways, temple walkways, public pedestrian zones, and driveways across India.

With proper installation on a sand-cement bed, Kota Stone pathways require virtually zero maintenance for decades — no resealing, no repainting, no periodic treatments needed.

Why Kota for Pathways

Key Advantages

All-Weather Durability

Handles India's extreme monsoon rains, intense summer heat, and winter frost without cracking, spalling, or surface degradation.

Maximum Slip Resistance

Natural finish Kota Stone has an R12 outdoor slip resistance rating — ensuring safe footing even in the heaviest rain.

Vehicle Load Capacity

30 mm thick Kota Stone withstands car and light vehicle traffic on driveways without cracking or shifting when properly installed.

Natural Aesthetics

Blends beautifully with garden landscaping, plants, and natural surroundings — far more attractive than concrete pavers.

Zero Maintenance

Unlike stamped concrete or wood decking, natural Kota Stone pathways need no resealing, staining, or periodic treatments.

Cost-Effective

Lower material and installation cost than most alternatives, with effectively zero maintenance cost over a 50+ year service life.
