About Kota Stone

What is Kota Stone?

Kota Stone is a fine-grained variety of limestone quarried in Kota district, Rajasthan. Known for its characteristic blue-green hue, extraordinary durability, and natural non-slip surface, it has been the material of choice for floors, pathways, and facades across India for centuries.

Its unique composition makes it resistant to weathering, chemicals, and heavy traffic — earning it the trust of architects, builders, and homeowners alike.

Textures & Surfaces

Finishes & Textures

Each finish transforms Kota Stone into a different character — from high-gloss luxury to rugged industrial strength.

Mirror Polish

Highly reflective surface achieved by multi-stage diamond polishing. Ideal for premium indoor flooring and lobbies.

Honed (Satin)

Smooth but non-reflective matte surface. Perfect for bathrooms, living rooms and contemporary interiors.

Natural / Rough

Original quarried surface with natural undulations. Best for outdoor use, terraces and rustic aesthetics.

Leather / Brushed

Wire-brushed texture that is slightly rough to touch. Great for pool surrounds and wet areas.

Flamed

Flame-treated for a deeply textured anti-slip surface. Commonly used on stairs, driveways and exteriors.

Sandblasted

Fine sand is blasted to create a uniform, lightly textured surface with a clean industrial look.
