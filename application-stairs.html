Kota Stone Staircases — Built to Last Generations

Staircases are one of the highest-stress elements in any building — they endure continuous concentrated point loads, heavy foot traffic, and edge abrasion that quickly degrade inferior materials. Kota Stone, with its exceptional 70+ MPa compressive strength and inherent toughness, is specifically suited for staircase applications.

Kota Stone stair treads are supplied in 40–50 mm thick slabs — providing the structural depth needed to span across a stair nosing without cracking or chipping. The stone's natural surface provides the anti-slip grip that building codes require, while its natural beauty elevates the visual character of any staircase.

From grand entrance staircases in 5-star hotels to practical, safe school steps, Kota Stone delivers performance, safety, and aesthetics that no other material can match at this price point. It is the trusted staircase material in thousands of Indian buildings across every sector.

Staircase Performance

Why Kota Stone for Stairs

Exceptional Structural Strength

70+ MPa compressive strength means Kota Stone tread slabs do not crack or split under point loads, even at high-traffic nosing zones.

Natural Anti-Slip Grip

All finishes except mirror-polished provide safe anti-slip traction on stair treads — naturally exceeding building code requirements.

No Chipping or Cracking

The dense, fine-grained limestone resists edge chipping — a critical requirement for nosing areas that receive the greatest daily impact.

Lifetime Serviceability

Unlike timber stairs that warp, or ceramic tiles that crack and detach, Kota Stone steps maintain their form and function indefinitely.

Architectural Beauty

The distinctive blue-green or brown natural stone creates striking stair elements that complement premium interior design aesthetics.

Low Maintenance

Sweep and mop only — no polishing, staining, or retreatment required. Stair treads can be spot-ground and re-honed on-site if needed.
