About This Variant

What is Kota Brown Stone?

Kota Brown is the warm, earthy counterpart to the iconic Kota Blue. Featuring rich brown, amber, and tan hues, it brings the comfort of natural stone to interiors and exteriors where warmth and rustic elegance are desired.

Quarried from the same geological formations in Kota, Rajasthan, Kota Brown shares the structural excellence of its blue sibling — identical compressive strength, low porosity, and long-term durability — while offering a dramatically different visual character that complements wood, terracotta, and heritage designs.

Kota Brown is especially popular for traditional Indian architecture, farmhouses, outdoor courtyards, and any project where a naturally warm, organic palette is desired.

Every slab carries unique natural veining and tonal variation — meaning no two installations are ever identical. A truly one-of-a-kind material.

Delivers a premium warm stone look comparable to expensive sandstone or travertine at a significantly lower investment.
