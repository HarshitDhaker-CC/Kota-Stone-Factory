Application

The Perfect Pool Decking Material

Pool surrounds have very demanding requirements — the surface must be anti-slip when wet, heat-resistant underfoot, moisture-durable, and visually beautiful in a sunlit outdoor setting. Kota Stone uniquely meets every single one of these requirements with no compromises.

Natural and leather finish Kota Stone provides R10 to R12 wet slip resistance — the highest safety rating for pool environments — dramatically reducing the risk of slipping accidents around the pool edge. Unlike ceramic tiles that become dangerously slippery when wet, Kota Stone's textured surface actually improves grip in wet conditions.

Kota Stone also stays cool to touch even in direct intense sunlight — unlike natural stone alternatives such as marble or granite that become uncomfortably hot. This is critical comfort for barefoot pool users on summer days.

Pool Performance

Why Kota Stone for Pools

Anti-Slip When Wet

R10–R12 rated wet slip resistance — the maximum safety classification. Maintains grip even under continuous water splash and wet bare feet.

Cool Underfoot in Summer

Kota Stone's thermal properties keep the surface significantly cooler than granite, marble, or dark-coloured tiles in direct summer sunlight.

Chlorine Resistant

Unaffected by pool water, chlorine splash, and pool chemicals — maintains colour and structural integrity without degradation.

UV Stable

The natural stone colour does not fade or bleach under UV exposure — maintaining its rich blue-green or brown tones for decades.

Beautiful Natural Look

The natural stone aesthetic complements water beautifully — creating a resort-like atmosphere that ceramic tiles and pavers cannot match.

Easy to Clean

Resists algae and mould when properly sealed. Simple pressure washing maintains a pristine pool deck appearance effortlessly.
